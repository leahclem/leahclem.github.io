My Background

My name is Leah Clemens, I am nineteen years old and the youngest of four. My older siblings have all attended NRCC before me and graduated with Associates Degrees. I still have one more year before I receive my Associates as well, and transfer to a four year college. My siblings and I grew up on a farm in Craig County. We had horses, cows, chickens, and several other animals of the Old MacDonald variety. I was home schooled up until 8th grade when I was enrolled at Dayspring Christian Academy. I spent two years there before I convinced my parents to let me attend public school. Sophomore year I transferred to Blacksburg High and later graduated with the class of 2016. After graduating, I made the decision to take a gap year before jumping straight into college. That year off helped me decide on pursuing a degree in Information Technology, and in Fall 2017 I enrolled at New River Community College.
